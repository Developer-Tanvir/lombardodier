lombard loans.

Our loans can help our private clients get projects off the ground, or finalise work underway. They can provide attractive private banking opportunities. A Lombard Loan is a fixed loan or agreed overdraft granted against a pledge of liquid assets such as equities, bonds or investment funds, up to a certain percentage of their value.

our offices.

Our roots are Swiss but we have an international outlook. For over two centuries, we have looked beyond our borders to offer our clients innovative and personalised private banking solutions. We were the first private bank to set up an office outside of Switzerland in 1951 and ever since, we’ve continued to find opportunities to expand our presence to better serve our clients, all around the world.
